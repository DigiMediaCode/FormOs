# FormOS Beta Outreach Pack

## 30-Second Pitch

FormOS is the form builder that finishes the job. It helps workflow businesses collect customer details, signatures, file uploads, and internal staff-only information, then turn the completed submission into a PDF record with files organized in Google Drive or Dropbox. It is built for rental, contractor, booking, and service businesses that have outgrown paper forms, PDFs, email attachments, and basic form tools.

## Objection Answers

### We already use Google Forms.

Google Forms is great for simple surveys and intake. FormOS is for workflows that need signatures, file uploads, office-only fields, final PDFs, storage routing, QR codes, and website embeds in one process.

### We already use PDF forms.

PDFs are familiar, but they often create manual work: downloading, filling, signing, emailing, storing, and renaming. FormOS keeps the customer form, uploads, signatures, internal fields, and final PDF in one workflow.

### We need legally binding agreements.

FormOS provides workflow templates and signature fields, but every business should review agreement wording for their jurisdiction and use case. The templates are strong starting points, not legal advice.

### We do not want another tool.

FormOS is designed to reduce the number of tools involved. Instead of using separate forms, email attachments, e-signature tools, spreadsheets, storage folders, and PDFs, FormOS puts the workflow together.

### Where do uploaded files go?

Form owners can connect Google Drive or Dropbox. Public submitters upload through the form, and files are routed to the owner's configured storage provider. FormOS stores limited metadata so the submission record stays organized.

### Can it work on our website?

Yes. FormOS supports public links, QR codes, iframe embeds, a WordPress plugin, and a Shopify theme app extension.

### Is it expensive?

The Free plan is for testing one simple workflow. Paid plans are positioned around business workflows, with trials available when enabled. The value is in reducing admin time, missing files, manual PDFs, and scattered records.

## 10-Minute Demo Call Script

### Minute 0-1: Context

"Before I show the product, how do you currently collect customer details, uploads, agreements, and internal staff checks?"

Listen for paper forms, PDFs, emails, WhatsApp/text messages, spreadsheets, and manual storage.

### Minute 1-2: Positioning

"FormOS is not a generic form builder. It is built for workflows where the form needs to produce a completed business record."

### Minute 2-4: Template

Open the relevant template landing page:

- Vehicle Hire Agreement.
- Equipment Rental Agreement.
- Contractor Job Intake + Waiver.
- Service Booking + Consent Form.
- Photography/Event Booking Agreement.

Show customer details, uploads, signatures, acknowledgements, and office fields.

### Minute 4-6: Builder and Publish

Show the builder briefly:

- Drag-and-drop fields.
- Collapsible field cards.
- Office Use Only fields.
- Conditional logic.
- Publish action.

### Minute 6-7: Customer Experience

Open the public form:

- Mobile-friendly layout.
- File upload.
- Signature.
- Submit.

### Minute 7-8: Back Office

Open submission detail:

- Customer answers.
- Uploaded file metadata.
- Office fields.
- Finalize PDF.
- Activity timeline.

### Minute 8-9: Sharing and Integrations

Show:

- Public link.
- QR code.
- Embed.
- WordPress/Shopify support.
- Google Drive/Dropbox storage.

### Minute 9-10: Feedback Ask

"Would this replace any current paper, PDF, email, or spreadsheet step in your process?"

"What would stop you from using this for one real workflow?"

"Would you be willing to try it with one form during beta?"
